# Surface Mouse Design

This project focuses on the design of a computer mouse using SolidWorks, mainly through surface modeling tools. The model was created by combining surface commands with a limited use of solid modeling features to achieve a more refined and visually appealing product form.

## Project Purpose

The purpose of this project was to improve my surface modeling skills in SolidWorks and to create a product design with smooth, continuous, and ergonomic surfaces. Although SolidWorks has certain limitations compared to dedicated industrial design and advanced surface modeling software, I aimed to achieve a clean and well-developed design result using the available tools.

## Software Used

- SolidWorks

## Design Approach

The mouse design was mainly developed using surface modeling commands. Surface tools were used to control the overall shape, curvature, and visual flow of the model. Some solid modeling commands were also used to support the design process and complete specific geometric details.

The design process focused on creating a form that is visually balanced, aerodynamic in appearance, and suitable for hand ergonomics. The main goal was to create a smooth outer body that feels natural in shape while also maintaining a modern product design language.

## Surface Modeling Features

- Surface-based body creation
- Smooth curvature transitions
- Form improvement through surface editing
- Aerodynamic and streamlined appearance
- Ergonomic shape development for hand compatibility
- Combination of surface and solid modeling techniques
- Visual refinement of the mouse body

## Design Considerations

During the modeling process, the surface geometry was improved step by step to achieve a better overall appearance. The outer form was shaped with attention to curvature continuity, hand placement, and product aesthetics.

Although the design can still be improved in terms of detailed manufacturability and functional component integration, the final model presents a strong visual concept and demonstrates effective use of SolidWorks surface modeling tools.
